Privacy Policy for 8bitChat

Effective Date: 01.01.2024

1. Data Collection

1.1 Chat Messages and User Logins

We do not log any chat messages or user logins. 8bitChat is designed to respect your privacy, and we do not store any personal data related to your conversations or login credentials.

1.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect non-personalized, aggregated data about how users interact with our website. This information helps us understand how users engage with 8bitChat, but it does not contain personally identifiable information.

1.3 CDNs

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to deliver assets and resources for a faster and more reliable user experience. The CDNs we use are:

  • nes.css CDN: We use the nes.css library from the unpkg.com CDN to provide styling for 8bitChat.
  • Press-Start-2p CDN: We use the Press-Start-2p font from fonts.cdnfonts.com to enhance the visual appearance of 8bitChat.

2. Data Usage

2.1 Chat Messages and User Logins

As mentioned earlier, we do not log or store any chat messages or user logins. Your conversations remain private and are not accessible to us or any third parties.

2.2 Google Analytics

The data collected through Google Analytics is used solely for the purpose of improving our website's performance and user experience. This data is anonymous and does not identify individual users.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies to track your activities on 8bitChat. However, third-party services like Google Analytics may use cookies as part of their data collection process. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about their use of cookies.

4. Third-Party Links

8bitChat may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external websites you visit through 8bitChat.

5. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your data:

  • Opting Out of Google Analytics: If you wish to prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on provided by Google.

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on the 8bitChat website or through other communication channels.
